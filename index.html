What's a Yakathon?

Yik Yak is looking to do some good in the world! This hackathon will be oriented towards social good.

People

When you're surrounded by some of the brightest minds in the world, there's no telling what you can accomplish! Feel free to come with a group, or we'll pair you with one if you don't have one.

Social Good

This is momentous. App Developer Club is teaming up with CS + Social Good and Yik Yak to bring you the first ever, Yakathon. Our focus for the hackathon will be innovating for social good.

Hacking

We'll have mentors on-site, from the board members of the App Developer Club and CS + Social Good, to respected developers and designers at Yik Yak.

Where is the Yakathon?

We'll be at Stanford University!
